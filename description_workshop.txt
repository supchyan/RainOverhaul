[h3]Atmospheric mod designed to make terraria more attractive.[/h3]

Includes 2 configurable rain modes to play with:
[list]
    [*][b]Ambient Mode[/b] - vanilla rain with customizable attractive filters.
    [*][b]Rain World Mode[/b] - something similar to Rain World game system with cycles and fall rain damage.
[/list]

[i]This mod supports multiplayer.[/i]
